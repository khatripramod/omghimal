Plan Your Trip

Visa Information

Most visitors can obtain a visa on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport. Fees vary based on length of stay:

  • 15 days: $30
  • 30 days: $50
  • 90 days: $125

Best Time to Visit

  • October-November: Peak season, clear skies
  • March-May: Spring, rhododendrons bloom
  • December-February: Cold but clear
  • June-September: Monsoon season

Essential Tips

  • Get travel insurance covering high altitudes
  • Book accommodations in advance during peak season
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly
  • Carry cash as cards aren't widely accepted
